# Freyja, Goddess of War and Magic

Freyja is more than a goddess of war—she's a symbol of life, death, and everything in between. On the battlefield, she commands not just swords, but Valkyries, magic, and raw, unyielding will. Fighting her isn't just combat—it's standing against the chaos of war itself, guided by the ancient wisdom.

## Running Freyja
- **Commanding Presence:** Freyja should feel untouchable at first, a war goddess orchestrating chaos while her Valkyries shield her from harm. Keep her on the move, using Seiðr magic to summon mist, curse, and empower.
- **Valkyrie Strategy:** Her Valkyries are not just fodder—they are extensions of her will. Use them to pressure the characters, forcing them to split their focus while Freyja rains down magic from a safe distance.
- **Final Fury:** Freyja's Last Stand should feel like the goddess herself has reached into her divine reserves to crush the characters. If they hesitate, her regenerative fury and Valkyrie summons will overwhelm them.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Brisingamen:** A necklace which grants its wearer not only unparalleled beauty but also the power to control emotions and summon divine protection.
- **Wand of Seiðr:** A magical wand that deals d8+3 damage. Once per day, Test Wits DR14 to cast a powerful spell. All allies gain advantage on defence for d3 rounds.
- **Freyja's Amulet:** A magical amulet that grants the wearer the ability to summon a Valkyrie once per day to aid in battle for d6 rounds.

## Attacks

### Högn's Blade
- **Damage:** d10+2
- **Effects:** Freyja's strikes with this blade are swift and purposeful, a seamless blend of elegance and brutality.

### Wand of Seiðr
- **Damage:** d8+3 ranged
- **Effects:** Freyja wields a magical wand, allowing her to cast bolts of Seiðr energy. If non-inscribed armour is hit, it is degraded one tier.

## Special

### Valkyries' Command
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Freyja calls upon the deadly spirits of Folkvangr to fight by her side, commanding their loyalty with the weight of eternity. Freyja summons 2 Valkyries to assist her in combat. If a Valkyrie is killed, Freyja can sacrifice d8HP to immediately summon another.

### Folkvangr's Blessing
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** The spirits of the honoured dead pour their power into Freyja and her allies, bolstering their will to fight. Freyja and her allies (including summoned Valkyries) gain +2 Armour and heal d6HP.

### Seiðr Magic
- **Uses:** Every round
- **Effect:** Freyja wields Seiðr magic with devastating precision, turning the battlefield into a theatre of chaos and control. Each round, she casts one magical effect:
  - **Mist of Folkvangr:** Freyja summons a thick, magical mist that obscures the battlefield. All enemies must Test Guile DR14 or attack with disadvantage for the next round.
  - **Goddess of Fertility:** Freyja blesses herself or an ally, granting +2 damage for the next round.
  - **Seiðr's Hex:** Freyja places a curse on a single target. The target must Test Fortitude DR14 or take d6 damage at the start of the next round and suffer disadvantage on defence and parry.

## Last Stand
- **Activates at:** HP < 20
- When Freyja's health drops below 20HP, her divine rage manifests, and she becomes even more dangerous.
  - Freyja gains an additional +2 to all PC defence.
  - She gains 2 Valkyries at the start of Last Stand.
  - While in this state, Freyja attacks twice per round.
  - When Freyja is defeated, her Valkyries also disappear.

### Battle Fury
- If an enemy is broken or dies from one of her attacks, she regains d6HP as her connection to the fallen warriors of Fólkvangr strengthens her.
