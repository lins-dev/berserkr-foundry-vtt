# Jörmungandr, the World Serpent

Every attack and ability should feel overwhelming, from the crushing power of its coils to the battlefield-altering effects of its presence. Let the players feel like they're battling a myth itself, one that shakes the very ground and sea around them.

## Running Jörmungandr
- **Battlefield Dominance:** Use Tidal Wave and Venomous Breath early to disrupt the party's positioning and weaken their cohesion.
- **Target Weaknesses:** Focus Venomous Bite on heavily armoured characters to degrade their defences, while using Constricting Coil to eliminate high-damage threats.
- **Endgame Fury:** Once Jörmungandr enters his final phase, his thrashes and Worldbreaker's Wrath should force the characters into a desperate scramble to avoid annihilation.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Fang of Jörmungandr:** A massive, venomous fang that can be fashioned into a weapon. It deals d10+4 damage and delivers a poison that causes d6 poison damage over 2 rounds.
- **Jörmungandr's Scales:** Thick, impenetrable scales that can be crafted into armour. Armour made from these scales grants +2 armour and resistance to poison (half damage from poison effects).
- **The Serpent's Blessing:** A blessing that grants the character immunity to poison and the ability to breathe underwater. Once per day, the character can summon a tidal wave which deals d8 damage and knocks enemies prone.

## Attacks

### Venomous Bite
- **Damage:** d12+6
- **Effects:** The bite delivers a potent venom. The target must Test Fortitude DR14 at the start of each round. Failure causes d6 damage and disadvantage on all Tests that round. On a hit, non-enchanted armour degrades by 1 tier.

### Tail Sweep
- **Damage:** d10+4
- **Effects:** Jörmungandr sweeps his massive tail across the battlefield, knocking down multiple enemies. All targets Test Swift DR12 or take d10+4 damage and be knocked prone.

## Special

### Venomous Breath
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Jörmungandr can unleash a cloud of poisonous gas from his maw, spreading across the battlefield and infecting anyone caught in it. All enemies caught in it must Test Fortitude DR14 or become poisoned.

### Tidal Wave
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** The serpent's presence disrupts the ocean itself, summoning a massive wave. Jörmungandr summons a massive wave that crashes across the battlefield. All creatures must Test Might DR14 or be swept away, taking d8 damage and being knocked prone.
- **Effect on Terrain:** After the wave hits, the battlefield becomes slippery and flooded, forcing all characters to Test Swift DR12 at the start of each round or move slower due to the difficult terrain. This effect lasts for d4 rounds.

### Constricting Coil
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Jörmungandr can wrap his massive body around multiple enemies, crushing them with his enormous strength. Jörmungandr targets up to 2 enemies, attempting to constrict them in his coils. The targets must Test Might DR14 to avoid being caught. If caught, the targets take 3d6 damage each round they remain constricted and must Test Might DR14 at the start of each round to break free. If they fail, they remain constricted and take additional damage each round until freed or killed.

## Ragnarök's End
- **Activates at:** HP < 30
- When Jörmungandr is severely injured he begins to thrash, making the battlefield even more dangerous. Any time he is successfully hit with a melee attack, Test Swift DR14 or take d12 damage.

### Worldbreaker's Wrath
- Jörmungandr can unleash his full power, striking the earth with his tail and causing catastrophic damage. The World Serpent can do this only once. Jörmungandr slams his massive tail into the ground, causing earthquakes that ripple across the battlefield. All enemies in battle must Test Fortitude DR16 or take 3d10 damage and be knocked prone. Additionally, the terrain becomes shattered and unstable, causing characters to Test Swift DR14 each round to avoid taking d6 damage from the broken ground.
