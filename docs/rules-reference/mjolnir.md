# Mjölnir

- **Type:** Warhammer
- **Damage:** d12+4 melee/ranged
- **Special:** Mjolnir automatically returns to the wielder's hand after being thrown.

## Abilities

### Thunderstrike (Every Round)
- Mjolnir calls upon the wrath of the storm to deal a powerful, lightning-infused strike. On a successful hit, Mjolnir deals an additional d6 lightning damage to the target. On a critical hit, the lightning arcs to nearby enemies, dealing d6 lightning damage to them as well.

### Hammer of the Gods (Once Per Battle)
- The power of the storm courses through Mjolnir, unleashing a devastating shock wave. The wielder smashes Mjolnir into the ground, releasing a shock wave that knocks all enemies close to the attacker prone. They must Test Fortitude DR14 or take 2d8 damage from the impact and thunderclap.

### Stormcaller (Once Per Day)
- The true power of Mjolnir lies in its ability to summon storms at the wielder's command. Once per day, the wielder can call down a storm that lasts for d6 rounds. During this time, the wielder gains +2 to all attacks, and lightning strikes randomly across the battlefield, dealing d8 lightning damage to a single enemy each round (GM's choice).

## Lore/Quest Hook (d6)

1. **Thor's Challenge:** Thor has noticed someone wielding his sacred weapon. He confronts the wielder, demanding a trial of strength and honour to prove their worthiness.
2. **Giants' Wrath:** The Jotnar have learned that Mjolnir has fallen into the hands of a mortal, and they send a group of Frost Giants to retrieve it, determined to avenge their countless defeats by Thor's hammer.
3. **Dwarven Claim:** The Dwarves Brokkr and Sindri demand Mjolnir's return, believing no one is worthy of wielding their creation except Thor. They send an emissary, offering a challenge: defeat them in a crafting contest or face their wrath.
4. **Storm Unleashed:** Uncontrolled use of Mjolnir draws the attention of Odin, who warns the wielder that such power comes with great responsibility. If the wielder continues abusing its power, they may face severe wrath.
5. **The Rival's Ambition:** Another powerful warrior seeks to claim Mjolnir for themselves, believing they are the true heir to Thor's might. They will stop at nothing to defeat the wielder and take the hammer for themselves.
6. **Thor's Favour:** Thor sends a Valkyrie to offer his blessing to the wielder if they use Mjolnir to complete a sacred quest in his name. Succeeding grants the wielder immunity to lightning damage.
