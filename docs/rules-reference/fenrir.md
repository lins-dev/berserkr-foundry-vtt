# Fenrir, the Great Wolf

Fenrir should be a terrifying, primal force—a predator that embodies the inevitability of death and vengeance. Every attack and ability should feel personal, like he's targeting each character with intent to destroy. Let the final phase turn into a chaotic, frenzied battle where the characters are fighting for their lives.

## Running Fenrir
- **Hunt and Destroy:** Use Pounce and Savage Bite to incapacitate key targets early. Fenrir should feel like he's always one step ahead, stalking and striking with precision.
- **Push the Pressure:** His Devour ability forces the party into high-stakes decisions, requiring teamwork to save swallowed allies while dealing with his relentless attacks.
- **Rage Unleashed:** Fenrir's Ragnarok's Rage phase should feel overwhelming. Use his double attacks and triple damage fumbles to create an intense, do-or-die finale.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Fang of Fenrir:** A massive fang that can be crafted into a weapon or enchanted for protection. As a weapon, it deals d12+6 damage.
- **Hide of Fenrir:** Thick, impenetrable hide that can be crafted into armour. Armour made from Fenrir's hide grants +3 armour and immunity to being knocked prone.
- **Fenrir's Essence:** This powerful essence can be used to imbue a character's weapon with Ragnarok's Rage, allowing them to double their damage (quadruple on a critical hit) for one battle. However, using the essence puts the wielder at risk of losing control. Test Wits DR12 to resist attacking the nearest ally.

## Attacks

### Savage Bite
- **Damage:** d12+6
- **Effects:** On a defence fumble, Fenrir deals an additional d6 damage and the target's armour is weakened, reducing its effectiveness by 1 tier.

### Claw Slash
- **Damage:** d8+4
- **Effects:** If Fenrir's claw attack lands, the target must Test Fortitude DR14 or begin to bleed (d4 damage per round until the character receives healing).

## Special

### Devour
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Fenrir's infamous attack can swallow enemies whole, turning the battlefield into a scene of desperate survival. Fenrir targets one enemy. The target must Test Swift or Might DR16 (player's choice) or be swallowed whole.
  - If swallowed, the character takes 4d6 damage each round from being crushed and suffocated within Fenrir's stomach.
  - The target can attempt to cut their way out by attacking from within; they must roll a critical hit or deal 18 damage in one round to escape. Allies can also help deal the 18 damage necessary in a round, or roll a critical hit from the outside to free them.
  - If the target is not freed within 4 rounds, they are killed outright. If they are considered a follower of Odin, the time limit is 2 rounds.

### Pounce
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Fenrir can leap massive distances, pinning a single enemy to the ground and savaging them with his claws. Test Might DR14 to avoid being pinned.
  - If pinned, the target takes d8+4 damage at the beginning of each round and must Test Might DR16 to break free.
  - While pinned, the pinned enemy defends with disadvantage.

## Ragnarok's Rage
- **Activates at:** HP < 25
- When Fenrir is wounded, he enters a berserk state, attacking anything in sight with uncontrollable fury.
  - The DR to defend increases by +2.
  - All attacks deal an additional d6 damage.
  - Fenrir attacks twice per round.
  - In this state, Fenrir is immune to all conditions except poisoned.
  - Defence fumbles against Fenrir deal triple damage instead of double.

### Fangs of Ragnarok
- Fenrir attacks with devastating speed and power, ensuring no escape for his prey. He can do this only once. Fenrir makes two attacks in a single round. Both attacks deal 2d12+6 damage. The second attack automatically hits any target that has already been pinned or swallowed.
