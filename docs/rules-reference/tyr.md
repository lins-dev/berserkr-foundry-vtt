# Tyr, God of War and Justice

Tyr is more than a warrior—he's a living symbol of sacrifice, duty, and the pursuit of balance. His presence on the battlefield is relentless and commanding, his every move carrying the weight of his divine purpose.

## Running Tyr
- **Relentless Combatant:** Tyr's approach should feel unrelenting. Use Sword of Tyr for heavy damage while keeping characters on edge with his Arm of Justice knockbacks.
- **Sacrificial Power:** Lean into Strike of Sacrifice when the moment calls for it, showing Tyr's willingness to put himself in harm's way to uphold justice.
- **Last Push:** When Tyr's Unyielding Resolve activates, ramp up the intensity. His final phase should make the characters feel the weight of his fury—he won't fall easily, and his every move demands their full focus.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Sword of Tyr:** This powerful sword deals d12+4 damage and, once per day, can deal an additional d12 damage on a critical hit.
- **Tyr's Ethereal Shield:** This shield grants +2 armour and, once per day, can be used to block all damage from a single attack.
- **Blessing of Justice:** This blessing allows the character to compel a single enemy to accept a holmgang. During the duel, the character gains +4 to attacks against the enemy.

## Attacks

### Sword of Tyr
- **Damage:** d12+4
- **Effects:** Tyr's sword strikes with overwhelming force, cutting through armour with ease. On a defence fumble, the sword deals an additional d6 damage and stuns the target for 1 round.

### Arm of Justice
- **Damage:** d6+2
- **Effects:** Tyr slams his fist into his opponent, knocking them back and disorienting them. The target must Test Fortitude DR12 or be knocked prone.

## Special

### Shield of Honour
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Tyr's unwavering sense of duty manifests as an ethereal shield, protecting both him and his allies. Tyr creates a divine shield that protects him and his allies for d4 rounds. This shield reduces all incoming damage by half for its duration.

### Strike of Sacrifice
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Tyr channels his belief in justice through a devastating strike, sacrificing his own strength to deliver a crushing blow. Tyr delivers a powerful blow with his sword that deals 3d12+4 damage. However, the force of the attack causes Tyr to suffer 2d8 damage himself as the strain of the strike takes its toll.

### Battlecry of Justice
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Tyr's voice carries across the battlefield, demoralizing his enemies and rallying his allies. Enemies within earshot must Test Fortitude DR14 or suffer -1 to all Tests for 2 rounds as they are demoralised by Tyr's unyielding strength and honour.

## Unyielding Resolve
- **Activates at:** HP < 20
- When Tyr's HP drops below 20, his resolve and sense of justice make him even more dangerous. He refuses to go down without a fight.
  - DR for defence increases by an additional +2.
  - His attacks deal an additional d6 damage.
  - Tyr attacks twice per round in this state, refusing to back down.
  - **Battle Surge:** For every enemy Tyr defeats, he regains d8HP (to a maximum of 20HP).

### Trial of Justice
- Tyr can call upon his divine authority as the god of justice to challenge a single enemy to a holmgang. He can do this only once. For the next 3 rounds, Tyr and the target are locked in combat. Both Tyr and the target gain +2 to all attacks against each other, and any attacks made by other enemies or allies during this duel are at disadvantage and risk incurring wrath of the gods.
