# Loki, the Trickster God

Loki's fight should be an exercise in chaos, forcing players to think quickly and adapt to ever-changing scenarios. From illusions to shape-shifting to reality manipulation, Loki's abilities embody her chaotic, mischievous nature, making the encounter as unpredictable as she is.

## Running Loki
- **Chaos and Unpredictability:** Shift forms frequently and use illusions to keep the party guessing. Loki should manipulate the battlefield as if it's an extension of her will, forcing characters to adapt constantly.
- **Mind Games:** Use illusions and phantoms to disorient and isolate characters, creating tension and mistrust within the group.
- **Final Phase:** When Loki enters Loki's Wrath, ramp up the chaos. Her dual forms and shifting terrain should make her feel like a force of pure entropy.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Lævateinn:** A dagger that deals d10+3 damage and multiplies into three shards when thrown, targeting up to three enemies. Once per day, it can bend reality, allowing the user to reroll a failed defence roll.
- **The Mask of Lies:** A cursed mask that grants the wearer automatic success on a Guile Test once per day. However, they are compelled to lie until the mask is removed.
- **Trickster's Omen:** A one-time-use relic that summons Fenrir (if alive) to fight alongside the character for d4 rounds.

## Attacks

### Lævateinn
- **Damage:** d10+3
- **Effects:** Loki throws a dagger that multiplies into three chaotic shards mid-air, each hitting a separate target (roll three attacks against different characters).

### Illusionary Claws
- **Damage:** d10+4 ranged
- **Effects:** Loki creates multiple illusory claws equal to the number of PCs, slashing at all enemies. All characters must Test Swift DR12 or take d6 damage.

## Special

### Trickster's Illusion
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Loki can create three illusions of herself, confusing the characters. The illusions move and attack alongside her, making it difficult to discern the real Loki.
  - Test Wits DR14 to identify the real Loki.
    - **Success:** The character can target the true Loki.
    - **Failure:** The character strikes an illusion, which disappears immediately.
  - The illusions have 0HP and deal no damage.

### Manipulation of Reality
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Once during the fight, Loki can bend reality to her will. Loki shifts the battlefield, changing the terrain, causing parts of the realm to collapse or conjuring dangerous environmental effects (such as fire, ice, or poisonous fog).
  - Test Swift DR14 to avoid environmental damage or take d6 damage.
  - Additionally, Loki can trap a single character in a phantasm—an illusory prison of the mind. The character must Test Wits DR16 to break free. Until they succeed, they are Stunned.

### Shape-shifting Chaos
- **Uses:** Once Per Round
- **Effect:** Loki can change her form each round, gaining different abilities based on her chosen form:
  - **Serpent Form:** Gains +2 Armour and her bite deals d8+3 poison damage. Injured characters must Test Fortitude DR12 or take an additional d6 poison damage at the start of the next round.
  - **Wolf Form:** Her bite deals d10 damage. If she hits a target, she may make an immediate second attack with Illusionary Claws for d6 damage.
  - **Crow Form:** Loki transforms into a crow and takes to the skies. She cannot be targeted by melee attacks.

## Loki's Wrath
- **Activates at:** HP < 20
- When Loki's HP drops below 20, her form begins to fracture and she becomes unhinged. Loki will start using two forms at once (e.g., Wolf + Serpent or Serpent + Crow). This grants her two attacks and dual special abilities each round.

### Fate's Trick
- Once during the fight, Loki can reverse the outcome of any single roll. She can turn a player rolled success into failure or a critical into a fumble. She might use this at a vital moment when the players think they've landed a final blow or when they are on the verge of defeating her.

### Reality Shakes
- The battlefield begins to break apart under her chaotic influence. Each round, the environment changes drastically, with areas catching fire, freezing over, or warping in impossible ways. Characters must Test Swift DR14 every round to avoid taking d6 damage from the shifting terrain.
