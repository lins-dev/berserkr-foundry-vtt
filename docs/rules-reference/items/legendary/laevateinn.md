# Lævateinn (Loki's Cursed Dagger)

- **Type:** Dagger
- **Damage:** d10+3
- **Special:** When thrown, the blade multiplies into three shards targeting up to three separate enemies.

## Abilities

### Infernal Flame (Passive)
- Laevateinn is constantly wreathed in hellish fire. On a successful hit, it deals an additional d6 fire damage and ignores 2 points of armour.

### Chaotic Surge (Once Per Battle)
- The blade surges with chaotic power, releasing a blast of destruction. Once per battle, the wielder can unleash a blast from Laevateinn, dealing 3d8 damage to all enemies close to the blade. The wielder must Test Fortitude DR14 or take d6 damage from the chaotic feedback.

### Curse of Fate (Passive)
- Each time the wielder lands a critical hit, they must Test Wits DR14. On a failure, they are cursed, suffering -2 to all Tests for the next d6 rounds as the blade corrupts their mind.
- If a fumble is rolled when attacking with Laevateinn, the wielder automatically takes 2d6 damage as the curse lashes out, and they lose d6HP permanently until a curse-breaking ritual is performed.

## Lore/Quest Hook (d6)

1. **Forged in Fire:** The fire giants of Muspelheim learn of Laevateinn's whereabouts and demand its return. Eldrimnir arrives to retrieve it by force.
2. **The Curse Deepens:** Laevateinn's curse worsens. The wielder experiences visions of Loki urging them to commit treachery, and they suffer from random blackouts during which their actions are not their own.
3. **Loki's Hand:** Loki herself becomes aware of the wielder and sends an emissary to make a pact: wield the blade in Ragnarok and gain untold power, or face her wrath for using it improperly.
4. **Binding Ritual:** A mysterious Dwarven craftsman offers to teach the wielder a ritual to bind Laevateinn's chaotic power, but it requires a blood sacrifice from an ally.
5. **Madness Beckons:** A group of berserkers seeks out Laevateinn, claiming it as their birthright. They will stop at nothing to retrieve it, believing it will drive them to greater blood-lust.
6. **The Flame Reformed:** Rumours circulate that the only way to control Laevateinn fully is to reforge it in the fires of Muspelheim. A treacherous journey through the Realm of Fire awaits.
