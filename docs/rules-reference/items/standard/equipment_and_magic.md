# Equipment & Magic - Berserkr

## Armor Tiers
- **Tier 1**: No armor. Flesh. Start with 1 random Rune.
- **Tier 2**: -d2 damage. (20‡)
- **Tier 3**: -d4 damage. +2DR Swift Tests. Defense is DR+2. (100‡)
- **Tier 4**: -d6 damage. +4DR Swift Tests. Defense is DR+2. (200‡)

## Runic Magic
- **Uses per day**: Roll **d4 + Guile**.
- **Inscribing**: Short rest. Consumes Rune. Permanent to item.
- **Casting**: Test **Wits DR12**. 
  - Success: Rune cast, use spent.
  - Failure: Fails, -1HP, use spent.
  - Fumble: Backfire (Favour/Wrath of Gods).

### Rune Sets (24 Total)
Divididos em Set 1 e Set 2 (12 cada). Exemplos:
- **Isa (Ice)**: Stun enemy or coat weapon in frost (d4 rounds, +d4 cold dmg).
- **Fehu (Wealth)**: Discern values or rain trinkets on target.
- **Berkano (Growth)**: Heal d6HP or healing aura.

## Legendary Weapons
- **Mjölnir**: d12+4. Returns to hand. Lightning arcs on crit.
- **Gungnir**: d10+5. Never misses. Critical ignores armor (+d8 dmg).
- **Lævateinn**: d10+3. Multiplies into 3 shards. Cursed fate on crit failure.
