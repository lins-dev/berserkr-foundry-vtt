# Heimdall, the Watchman of the Bifrost

Heimdall isn't just a guardian—he's the quiet power of duty, the immovable resolve of a protector who watches the tides of fate crash against his station. Every swing of Hofund, every step he takes, is deliberate, precise, and unshakable. To fight him is to face the certainty of a being who will not falter, even at the end.

## Running Heimdall
- **Heightened Senses:** Heimdall isn't just a fighter—he's a watcher, always one step ahead. Use Guardian's Sight to frustrate ranged attackers and make Heimdall feel untouchable.
- **Control the Field:** Abilities like Gjallarhorn and Blinding Radiance make Heimdall a master of crowd control. Blind, stun, and summon allies to overwhelm the party while Heimdall methodically picks them apart.
- **Final Stand:** Heimdall should fight hardest when he's at his weakest. Lean into his Final Stand, making the players feel like they're battling an immovable force. His Oath should be a last, terrifying reminder of his dedication to Asgard.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Gjallarhorn:** This legendary horn can be blown once per day to stun all enemies within earshot for one round. Test Wits DR14 to activate.
- **Hofund:** A powerful sword that deals d10+3 damage. Once per day, it can release a shock wave, knocking enemies close to it prone.
- **Heimdall's Sight:** A blessing that grants the wielder the ability to see through illusions and spot hidden enemies. Once per day, the character can automatically avoid an incoming attack by predicting it.

## Attacks

### Hofund, The Guardian's Sword
- **Damage:** d10+3
- **Effects:** Heimdall's sword, Hofund, glows with divine energy. On a defence fumble, the sword releases a shock wave, knocking back all enemies. Test Swift DR16 or be knocked prone.

### Blinding Radiance
- **Damage:** d6+4 ranged
- **Effects:** Heimdall can channel the radiant energy of the Bifrost, releasing a blinding light. Enemies close to Heimdall must Test Guile DR12 or gain blindness for 1 round.

## Special

### Horn of Gjallarhorn
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** When Heimdall blows Gjallarhorn, the sound shakes reality itself, calling Vakten to his aid and striking fear into all who hear it.
  - Heimdall summons d4 Vakten.
  - The sound of Gjallarhorn causes all enemies that can hear to Test Wits DR14 or be stunned for 1 round.

### Guardian's Sight
- **Uses:** Every round
- **Effect:** Heimdall's all-seeing eyes pierce through shadows and deceptions. He anticipates attacks before they even begin. Increase the DR to hit by +2 for one ranged attack per round, as he sees it coming before it happens.

### Bifrost Strike
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** A searing beam of the Bifrost descends from the heavens at Heimdall's command, tearing through his enemies like a wrathful god's judgement. Heimdall summons a beam of Bifrost from the sky, targeting up to two enemies. Each must Test Swift DR14 or take d8+2 damage. If the target is wearing non-inscribed armour, they suffer disadvantage on their next defence.

### Battle Sense
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Heimdall's unmatched awareness turns him into a whirlwind of divine precision. Once per battle, Heimdall can choose to act thrice in one round, making three attacks in a single round.

## Final Stand
- **Activates at:** HP < 15
- When pushed to his limit, Heimdall becomes an unyielding wall of defence, embodying Asgard's last hope.
  - Heimdall gains +2 armour.
  - He adds an additional +2 DR to all PC defence.
  - Heimdall also attacks twice per round while in this state, refusing to go down without a fight.

### Heimdall's Oath
- If defeat is certain, Heimdall sacrifices himself in a final, devastating blow. All enemies close to him take 4d6 damage, his dying act a testament to his unwavering duty.
