# Thor, God of Thunder

Thor isn't just a warrior—he's the storm itself. Every attack should feel like a hammer blow from the heavens, and every roar of thunder a reminder that even gods tremble when he fights. When he enters his berserk state the players will understand why he's the defender of Asgard and a force that no mortal—or immortal—takes lightly.

## Running Thor
- **The Storm Unleashed:** Thor's attacks should feel relentless, like a thunderstorm bearing down on the party. Use Mjolnir's Strike and Thunderous Charge to keep the party constantly on the defensive.
- **Wrath Personified:** When Thor takes heavy damage, Wrath of the Thunderer brings devastating payback.
- **Unstoppable Rage:** Thor's final phase is his deadliest. Combine his double attacks with Mjolnir's Wrath to make his berserk state a desperate race for survival.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Mjolnir:** The fabled hammer of Thor, renowned for its unrelenting strength and its ability to summon the power of the storm.
- **Thor's Gauntlets:** These gauntlets grant the wearer +2 armour and allow them to deal an additional d6+2 damage. Once per day, the wearer can call down lightning to strike a single target for d8 damage.
- **Belt of Strength:** This belt increases the wearer's Might by +2 and allows them to perform one Thunderous Charge per day, knocking enemies prone and dealing d10+3 damage.

## Attacks

### Mjolnir's Strike
- **Damage:** d12+4 melee/ranged
- **Effects:** Thor swings Mjolnir with all his might. On a successful hit, lightning splits from the hammer to the nearest enemy. Test Swift DR14 or take d10 damage. Thor can also throw his hammer, which always returns to his hand.

### Thunderous Charge
- **Damage:** d10+3
- **Effects:** Thor charges into battle with thunderous force, smashing into his enemies. All enemies in his path must Test Swift DR14 or be knocked prone. Thor can make one follow-up attack after the charge against a single prone target, dealing an additional d6+2 damage.

## Special

### Summon Lightning
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Thor calls down the full force of the storm to obliterate a single foe. Thor calls down a blast of lightning to strike the enemy with the longest Saga. The enemy must Test Fortitude DR16 or take d100 damage.

### Wrath of the Thunderer
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** When Thor is dealt 10 or more damage in a single blow, the attacker must Test Might DR14; failure means Thor retaliates with Mjolnir's Strike immediately.

### Defender of Asgard
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** As the protector of Asgard, Thor instinctively defends himself or his allies, using Mjolnir to block attacks or retaliate against enemies that threaten those under his protection. Once per battle, when Thor or an ally is attacked, Thor can use Mjolnir to deflect the blow. The attacker must Test Might DR14; on a failure, the attack is completely negated and Thor can immediately make a retaliatory strike against the attacker using Mjolnir's Strike.

## Unstoppable Rage
- **Activates at:** HP < 25
- When Thor is severely injured, his berserk rage takes over, making him nearly unstoppable.
  - Attack DR to hit Thor increases by +2.
  - Thor gains +4 to all damage rolls and attacks twice per round.
  - Thor becomes immune to all conditions.
  - He regenerates d6HP every time he lands a hit (to a maximum of 25HP).

### Mjolnir's Wrath
- In moments of dire need, Thor can call upon the full power of Mjolnir, channelling its divine energy in a single, all-consuming strike that shakes the very heavens and earth. He can do this only once and only during his Unstoppable Rage. Thor targets all enemies he can see and unleashes a devastating blow, striking with lightning-infused fury. This attack ignores non-enchanted armour, dealing 4d10+5 lightning damage to each target.
