# Frigg, Queen of Asgard

Frigg isn't just a combatant—she's inevitable. Every move she makes feels deliberate, as though the battle has already been written, as her threads tighten around the players' choices. Fighting her is like fighting fate itself—unforgiving, unrelenting, and inescapable.

## Running Frigg
- **Master of Control:** Frigg's abilities make her the puppet master of fate. Use Weaver of Fate to force rerolls, keeping players guessing and robbing them of certainty.
- **Defensive Precision:** Frigg isn't a brawler; she uses her Blessing to shield herself and her allies, while softening up enemies with curses and entangling threads. Play her as untouchable, always a step ahead.
- **Unstoppable in the Last Prophecy:** When her Last Prophecy triggers, Frigg becomes almost untouchable. Use this moment to turn the battlefield into a web of fate, overwhelming characters with her control and pushing them to act quickly before her visions consume them entirely.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Spindle of Fate:** A magical spindle that grants its wielder limited control over destiny. Once per day, it allows the user to reroll any unsuccessful Test, pulling the strings of fate in their favour.
- **Frigg's Cloak of Woven Fate:** A cloak woven from threads of destiny, granting +2 armour and the ability to foresee danger once per session (automatically negating a Wrath of the Gods effect).
- **Blessing of Frigg:** A divine boon that shields the bearer from death's grasp. Once per session, upon death, the character is instead restored to 1HP, protected by Frigg's foresight.

## Attacks

### Spindle of Fate
- **Damage:** d8+2
- **Effects:** Frigg throws enchanted threads of fate that twist and coil around her enemies. On a defence fumble, the target is entangled in the threads and stunned for 1 round.

### Woven Curse
- **Damage:** d6+3 ranged
- **Effects:** Frigg curses her enemies with a spoken word of power. The target must Test Fortitude DR12 or suffer disadvantage on their next attack as the curse disrupts their focus.

## Special

### Weaver of Fate
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Frigg pulls the threads of destiny taut, forcing the flow of events to bend to her will. Frigg can force a player to reroll any successful Test once per battle.

### Frigg's Blessing
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** As the protector of life and the matron of wisdom, Frigg can shield herself or an ally with divine power. Frigg grants +3 armour to herself or an ally for 3 rounds, shielding them from harm with the power of fate. In addition, if the target dies during this time, they are restored to 1HP.

### Threads of Destiny
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Frigg binds her enemies with strands of fate, or cuts them off entirely from the flow of destiny. Frigg targets a character and forces them to Test Wits DR16.
  - **Success:** They resist, but are still bound by fate and suffer -2 to all Tests for the next 2 rounds.
  - **Failure:** The target is temporarily cut off from fate and cannot receive any healing, magical buffs, or divine intervention (Fates) for the next 3 rounds.

## The Last Prophecy
- **Activates at:** HP < 20
- When Frigg's health drops below 20HP, her foresight sharpens and she becomes one with the weave of fate. Frigg sees every outcome for the next 3 rounds. The DR to hit her is increased by an additional d6.

### Fate's Chosen
- Frigg selects a champion, turning fate against her foes by bending their will to her purpose. She can do this only once. Frigg selects one of her enemies to fight for her and grants them automatic success on their next Test. Test Wits DR16 or be compelled to fight for Frigg for d4 rounds.
