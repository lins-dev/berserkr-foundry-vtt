# Hel, Goddess of Death

Hel's presence on the battlefield should embody the inevitability of death—relentless, draining, and always in control. Her abilities to weaken the party, replenish her forces, and regenerate in her final phase create an uphill battle for the players, forcing them to plan carefully and strike decisively.

## Running Hel
- **Master of the Dead:** Use Raise the Dead early to bolster Hel's defences and overwhelm the characters. Her abilities should keep the party on edge, forcing them to divide their attention.
- **Control the Battlefield:** Combine Aura of Despair with attacks like Soul Bolt to sap the party's strength and morale. Hel thrives on wearing her enemies down slowly, staying in control of the encounter.
- **Final Stand:** When Ruler of Helheim activates, Hel becomes a dire threat. Use her regeneration and boosted attacks to push the characters into a desperate battle for survival.

## Loot and Rewards
- **Hel's Sickle:** A curved, necrotic blade that deals d8+3 cold damage. Any damage dealt with this weapon heals the user for the amount of damage dealt.
- **Hel's Cloak:** A cloak woven from the essence of death itself. It grants +2 armour. Once per day, the wearer can become intangible for d4 rounds, allowing them to move through objects and enemies. They also gain advantage on all defence for the duration of the effect.
- **The Blessing of Hel:** One time use. Activating this blessing allows the user to deal double damage to all Followers of Loki for one battle.

## Attacks

### Chilling Touch
- **Damage:** d8+3
- **Effects:** On a successful hit, the target must Test Fortitude DR14 or lose 2 Might for the remainder of the encounter.

### Soul Bolt
- **Damage:** d10+4 ranged
- **Effects:** Necrotic energy siphons life force from the victim, healing Hel for the damage dealt.

## Special

### Raise the Dead
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Hel can summon the souls of the dead to fight on her behalf, bolstering her forces with spectral warriors. Hel summons d4+1 Modgudr's Sentinels to fight for her. While the undead are active, +2 DR to attacks made against Hel.

### Death's Embrace
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** A touch that freezes the soul. Hel targets one enemy. The target must Test Fortitude DR16 or be stunned for d4 rounds, unable to act as the freezing embrace of death overtakes them. If the target succeeds, they take d8 cold damage but are not stunned.

### Aura of Despair
- **Uses:** Once Per Battle
- **Effect:** Hel exudes an aura of hopelessness and despair, sapping the will of those who oppose her. All enemies she can see must Test Wits DR14 or suffer disadvantage when attacking her for d6 rounds as they are overwhelmed by despair.

## Ruler of Helheim
- **Activates at:** HP < 20
- When Hel is gravely wounded, she draws power from her dominion over the underworld, becoming more dangerous as she taps into the power of the dead.
  - Once Hel's HP drops below 20, she attacks twice per round.
  - She also causes an additional +2 to all defence Tests.
  - She regenerates d6HP per round for the next 3 rounds (to a maximum of 20HP) as she draws power from the souls of Helheim.
  - **Death's Vengeance:** During this time, all of Hel's attacks deal an additional d6 damage as her connection to the underworld strengthens.

### The Reaping
- Hel calls upon the dead to reap the souls of her enemies, dealing devastating damage to all who oppose her. She can do this only once. Hel unleashes a wave of necrotic energy across the battlefield. All enemies must Test Fortitude DR16 or take 3d10 damage. If the Test is failed, one random Rune is removed from a weapon or armour.
