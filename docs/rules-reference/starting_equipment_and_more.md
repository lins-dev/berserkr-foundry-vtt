## Armour

Your armour. Roll d4 to determine the tier, then choose an armour type from that tier.

**TIER 1:** No Armour. Characters with no armour start with one random Rune.

**TIER 2:** Reduces damage taken by d2. Cost: 20t
- Worn Leather Jerkin: A weathered leather garment, softened by countless battles and travels.
- Fur-Lined Cloak: A warm cloak lined with thick furs, providing comfort and protection against the elements.
- Padded Gambeson: A quilted jacket, worn beneath armour or on its own, offering a layer of defence against blows.

**TIER 3:** Reduces damage taken by d4 and imposes a +2DR penalty on Swift Tests. Cost: 100t. Defence is DR+2.
- Studded Leather Cuirass: A hardened leather vest reinforced with metal studs, offering decent protection while maintaining mobility.
- Chainmail Hauberk: A shirt of interlocking metal rings, providing reliable defence against slashing attacks.
- Scale Armour: A flexible armour of overlapping metal scales, offering a balance of protection and agility.

**TIER 4:** Reduces damage taken by d6, imposes a +4DR penalty on Swift Tests. Cost: 200t. Defence is DR+2.
- Lamellar Cuirass: A segmented cuirass of lacquered metal plates, meticulously arranged like protective scales.
- Plate Hauberk: A coat of overlapping iron plates, forged into a rigid shell that encases the wearer in steel.

**Repairing Armour:** Armour cannot be repaired to a tier higher than its original but can be upgraded by a smith.
- Tier 2 to 3: 100t, 1 Day
- Tier 3 to 4: 200t, 2 Days
