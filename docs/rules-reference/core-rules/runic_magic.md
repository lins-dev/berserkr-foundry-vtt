# Runic Magic

RUNIC MAGIC is not just a force—it's a language. The gods carved the Runes into the very fabric of reality, and those who understand these symbols can tap into their ancient power.

Runes aren't just a list of spells; they're pieces of a puzzle. However, Runes carry risk, and using them incorrectly can have dire consequences.

## Using Runes
Runes can either be inscribed or cast as spells.

### Daily Limit
After a long rest, roll **d4 + Guile** to determine how many times you can use Runes that day. These uses are spent when casting spells or activating inscribed Runes.

### Inscribing
Inscribing a Rune onto a piece of armour or a weapon takes a short rest—you must still use food and water but forego regaining any HP. After inscription, that Rune may not be used as a spell or inscribed again. It is destroyed in the process and the inscription cannot be undone.

### Casting
To use a Rune, **Test Wits DR12** to see if you successfully tap into its power.
- **Success:** You cast the Rune's effect, consuming one of your daily uses.
- **Failure:** The spell fails. You lose 1HP and consume one of your daily uses.

### Fumble
The Rune backfires, causing unpredictable or devastating effects. Invoke Favour or Wrath of the Gods.

### Rolling a Random Rune
Since there are 24 runes, roll **d2** to determine the set of runes (set 1 or set 2), and then roll **d12** to select a rune from that set.

### Set 1 (Fehu – Wunjo)
| Rune | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Fehu** (Wealth) | For the next d4 hours, your ability to discern valuable objects, both hidden and in plain sight, is heightened. Gain +2 to any Test involving Trinkets or valuables. |
| **Uruz** (Strength) | Channel the raw energy of Uruz, gaining or bestowing +2 to Might Tests for d6 rounds. |
| **Thurisaz** (Giant) | Unleash a shock wave from your weapon, knocking all enemies close to you prone and dealing d4 damage to each. |
| **Ansuz** (Message) | Invoke insight from the Norns to gain a truthful answer to a question or problem. |
| **Raido** (Journey) | Know the most direct path to your destination or summon a gate, without the need for a ritual, a totem or Test. |
| **Kenaz** (Fire) | Brand an enemy with Kenaz's Rune, causing them blindness and also making them glow brightly, making them visible through walls, darkness, or magical concealment. Attacks against the branded enemy are made with advantage. |
| **Gebo** (Gift) | Exchange life force. Heal an ally for d6HP at the cost of your own HP. |
| **Wunjo** (Joy) | Emit a joyful song that heals all nearby allies for d4HP and grants +1 to their next Test. |
