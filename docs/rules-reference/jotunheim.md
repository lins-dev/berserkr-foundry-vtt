# Jotunheim: Realm of the Giants

Jotunheim, the vast, frozen land of giants, has been consumed by a relentless frost, but the biting cold is only the start. Towering ice cliffs rise like jagged teeth, and shifting glaciers turn the landscape into a treacherous maze. Hidden beneath layers of snow, deep crevasses wait to swallow the unwary, leaving only silence behind.

The Jotnar, once proud and solitary, have grown more aggressive as Fimbulwinter grinds on. Resources, already scarce, have dwindled further, sparking brutal skirmishes. The strongest giants now dominate, forcing weaker clans into submission or driving them from their strongholds. Once unified, the Jotnar are now fractured, forging uneasy alliances out of desperation, destined to crumble as quickly as the ice beneath their feet.

Meanwhile, monstrous creatures prowl the frozen wastes. Frost drakes glide through the snow, and colossal ice beasts, driven from their lairs, hunt anything in their path, including giants.

## Weather (d6)

1. **Blinding Blizzard**: A blizzard sweeps through the land, reducing visibility to nothing. All movement is halved, and the party must Test Fortitude DR12 every hour to avoid taking d4 cold damage from the extreme cold.
2. **Reshaping Landscape**: The glacier beneath the party shifts, creating new paths and revealing hidden features in the ice. What was once impassable now offers potential shortcuts. The party may Test Wits DR12 to spot a newly opened route or hidden cave.
3. **Howling Winds**: The wind howls through the ice cliffs, creating a deafening roar. All Guile Tests are made with disadvantage for the next hour, and attacks suffer a -2 penalty as the winds tear through the air.
4. **Frozen Fog**: A thick fog rolls in, freezing everything it touches. The party must Test Fortitude DR12 or suffer from frostbite, which deals d4 damage and slows movement by half until treated.
5. **Dance of Ice**: Tiny, glittering ice crystals fall from the sky in a mesmerizing display, reflecting the pale light of Jotunheim. The air feels oddly calm despite the cold.
6. **Fimbulwinter's Breath**: An unnatural cold settles over the land, even more intense than usual. All Fortitude Tests are made with disadvantage for the next day, and anyone who fails these Tests suffers d4 cold damage.

## Discoveries (d6)

1. **Frozen Corpse**: A giant's body, frozen solid, half-buried in the snow. Clutched in its massive hand is a giant-sized weapon or some Trinkets—perhaps a rune-carved weapon—too cold to touch without feeling your bones crack. Test Fortitude DR12 to pry it from his cold, dead hands.
2. **Ancient Giant's Relic**: Deep in the ice, you find some old giant's relic. A weapon, an item, maybe a Rune. Wielding it feels like holding a lightning bolt in your hand. But those Jotnar will notice.
3. **Abandoned Frost Drake Nest**: The party discovers a Frost Drake's nest, long abandoned. Inside are broken eggshells and remnants of their prey, but one unhatched egg remains...
4. **Cracked Ice Bridge**: A natural bridge of ice stretches across a deep ravine. Crossing it requires you to Test Wits DR12 to find the safest path, but halfway across the ice begins to crack. Anyone failing to Test Swift DR14 falls into the chasm below.
5. **Mysterious Cave**: The party stumbles across a hidden cave, sheltered from the storm. Inside, they find the remains of a giant's Trinket hoard, though it's guarded by an ice trap (d6 damage). Test Wits DR10 to notice the trap and Test Guile DR14 to disarm it.
6. **Frozen Waterfall**: A majestic frozen waterfall stands tall in the distance, its icy columns glittering in the faint light. Hidden behind it is a secret chamber filled with Trinkets, Runes and a long-forgotten weapon that may be able to help topple the Frost King.

## Rumours (d6)

1. "Thrym's building an army." Thrym, the Frost King, is raising an army, one that could break the sky. They say he's found a way to control the blizzards tearing apart Jotunheim, and he ain't gonna use that power for a winter holiday.
2. "The mountain's groaning." It ain't just the cold shaking the land. Giants are talking about something old, something asleep beneath those frozen peaks, waking up like a beast with a hunger for chaos. Some think it's Ragnarok knocking at the door. Others? They say it's worse—something even Thrym's too scared to wake.
3. "Geirrod's laying traps for Thor." Geirrod's been setting traps—traps so damn clever the gods themselves might fall for 'em. Giants who wander into his part of Jotunheim? They don't come out the same, if they come out at all. Even the gods know better than to cross that bastard.
4. "The Frost Drakes are restless." The Frost Drakes—always aloof, always distant—are getting hungry. And mean. Now, they're even taking swipes at giants, looking for something. Mad from the cold? Or just pissed they don't own this place anymore?
5. "The Rime Etins are ready to burn it all down." The Rime Etins, those smaller giants, are done with being Thrym's puppets. They've got plans to overthrow the big man, but to do it, they need blood—and plenty of it. You help them? You're marked for death, too, and Thrym's not one for mercy.
6. "The glaciers are waking." Jotunheim's glaciers? They've started moving like they've got somewhere to be. Giants can't agree on why—some blame the Bergresi, some think it's Geirrod stirring shit, but most know deep down: if the ice starts moving like that, Ragnarok's closer than anyone wants to admit.

## Encounters (d8)

1. **Frost Drake Attack**: A Frost Drake swoops down from the icy peaks, its wings slicing through the icy mist. With a screech, it dives from the sky, unleashing a blast of freezing breath. The air turns to ice in an instant.
2. **Rime Etin Patrol**: A group of d3 Rime Etins, towering figures wreathed in frost, patrol the area with an air of pompous vigilance. They sense the party's presence and, with a clap of their massive hands, summon a storm of jagged ice spears. The freezing missiles rain down, dealing d6 damage. Test Swift DR12 to dodge the deadly barrage.
3. **Glima Giant Challenge**: A Glima Giant approaches, its hulking form casting a shadow over the party. It challenges them to a contest of might—combat or wrestling.
4. **Bergresi Uprising**: The ground trembles as a group of d2 Bergresi, mountain spirits, rise from the earth. A rock slide roars toward the party, forcing them to Test Swift DR12 to avoid being buried. Hostile to outsiders, the Bergresi can be appeased with offerings to the mountain.
5. **Thrym's Scouts**: The party is spotted by a group of Rime Etins (equal to the number of PCs), loyal to Thrym, the Frost King. They present a grim choice—serve Thrym in his preparations for Ragnarok or face their wrath.
6. **Geirrod's Trap**: The cunning giant Geirrod has set a maze of traps across the icy cliffs. The party must Test Guile DR14 to spot them and Test Swift DR14 to avoid them. Failure leads to rock slides, pitfalls, or worse. Geirrod lurks in the shadows, waiting to strike those who fall into his snares.
7. **Frozen Crevasse**: The party stumbles upon a deep crevasse hidden beneath the snow. They must Test Wits DR14 to find a safe path across, or risk a deadly fall. Inside, forgotten treasure may await.
8. **Thrym, the Frost King**: The party encounters Thrym, the Frost King, standing tall atop a glacier, his cold gaze sweeping over the land. He offers them a choice: kneel before him and serve in Ragnarok's battle, or prepare to fight.

## Creatures of Jotunheim

### Frost Drakes
- **HP:** 9
- **Armour:** -
- **Weapon:** Ice breath d6 ranged, Claw d4
- **Special:** Fly. Can fly at will. Frozen Breath. Once per encounter, the Frost Drake can unleash its icy breath in a cone, dealing d6 damage to all targets caught in its path. Affected targets must Test Might DR12 or be stunned for one round.

### Rime Etins
- **HP:** 12
- **Armour:** -
- **Weapon:** Ice spear d6, Ice club d8
- **Special:** Summon Blizzard. Once per encounter, the Rime Etin can conjure a blizzard. All enemies close to the Etin must Test Fortitude DR14 or take d4 damage and gain a -2 penalty to Swift Tests for the next round.

### Bergresi
- **HP:** 3
- **Armour:** Stone skin - d4
- **Weapon:** Rock fists d8
- **Special:** Stone Merge. A Bergresi can move through rock and stone, making it impossible to hit while within solid terrain. It can emerge to attack, leaving it vulnerable, before retreating back into the rock during the next round.

### Glima Giants
- **HP:** 15
- **Armour:** Giant's skin - d2
- **Weapon:** Choke slam d10
- **Special:** Wrestling Moves. Roll a d3 each round: 1=Suplex (deals d6 damage and knocks target prone); 2=Bear Hug (deals d4 damage each round until target escapes, Test Might DR12 to break hold); 3=Throw (throws target a short distance, dealing d6 damage and leaving them stunned).

### Thrym, the Frost King
- **HP:** 30
- **Armour:** Ice-encrusted body - d3
- **Weapon:** Ice club of Thrym d12+4
- **Special:** Ice Men Cometh. Thrym calls forth ice-formed warriors from the snow. Roll d4 to determine how many Ice Warriors appear (4HP, icy fists d6). Blizzard Summon. Once per encounter, summon a blizzard. All creatures (not Ice Warriors) Test Fortitude DR14 or take d6 cold damage and -2 to all Tests until end of next turn.
- **Rewards:** Ice Club of Thrym (d12+4 damage).

### Geirrod
- **HP:** 22
- **Armour:** Cloak of the Jotunn - d2
- **Weapon:** Bear trap sling d4 ranged (Test Swift DR12 or be unable to move during the next round)
- **Special:** Lair of Traps. In his lair, party must Test Swift DR14 to avoid traps; failure causes d4 damage. Shadow in the Rocks. Once per encounter, vanish into rock formations, becoming untargetable for one round. When he re-emerges, his next attack gains +2DR to defence.
- **Rewards:** Geirrod's Master Trap. One-use trap, almost impossible to spot by Jotnar, if caught target takes d12+3 damage and cannot move during the next round.
