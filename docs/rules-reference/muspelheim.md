# Muspelheim: Realm of Fire

Muspelheim is a world born of fire, where flames rule everything. Rivers of molten lava cut through the land, casting a red glow over jagged volcanic peaks. At the centre, a massive volcano erupts endlessly, spewing molten rock and ash into the boiling sky. The heat here is suffocating, pressing down like a physical weight, while red lightning cracks through the darkness, momentarily lighting up the chaos.

Nothing stays still. The volcano reshapes the land constantly—fresh lava breaks open the ground, steam bursts from fissures, and molten boulders fall from the sky like the gods' fury. The ground trembles under it all, a constant pulse that runs through the realm, as if the earth itself is struggling under the weight of endless fire. Lava moves beneath the surface like blood, ready to erupt at any moment.

Even the air is alive with fire, ash swirling with flames, distorting vision, blurring the line between solid ground and raging inferno. There is no escape—no shelter from the relentless heat. The flames consume everything, and the ground seems hungry for destruction.

## Weather (d6)

1. **Ash Storm**: A thick storm of ash covers the land, reducing visibility to almost nothing. All Swift Tests are made with disadvantage, and the ash clogs your lungs, forcing you to Test Fortitude DR12 every hour or take d4 damage and become Fatigued.
2. **Molten Rain**: Molten rock rains from the sky as the volcanoes spew fiery debris. The party must Test Swift DR14 to dodge the falling chunks of lava, or suffer d6 damage from the intense heat. The rain lasts for d4 hours, making travel dangerous.
3. **Blistering Mirage**: The heat becomes so intense that heat waves form, distorting the landscape into surreal and shifting visions.
4. **Red Lightning Storm**: The sky flashes with red lightning, striking randomly across the landscape. The party must Test Swift DR12 to avoid being struck by a bolt, which deals d6 damage.
5. **Volcanic Tremors**: The ground shakes violently as the volcanoes rumble, causing the terrain to shift and crack. The party must Test Swift DR12 to avoid falling into the newly formed crevasses, which lead straight into rivers of molten rock.
6. **Cleansing Ember Winds**: A hot, ember-laden wind sweeps through the land, burning away impurities. The party feels momentarily invigorated, gaining a +2 bonus to Fortitude Tests for the next day.

## Discoveries (d6)

1. **Abandoned Forge**: The party stumbles upon an old Dwarven forge, its walls scorched by flames long past. Reactivating the forge triggers a deadly lava eruption. The party must Test Swift DR12 to avoid disaster.
2. **Molten Pool**: A pool of molten rock pulses with faint magical energy. Dipping weapons into the molten pool imbues them with fire damage (additional d6 for d4 days), but it's a risky move. The party must Test Fortitude DR12; failure destroys the weapon in the process.
3. **Fire-Resistant Ore Vein**: A vein of rare fire-resistant ore gleams in the rock, perfect for mining Runes. Mining it requires you to Test Might DR12, but the noise may draw the attention of Molten Hounds lurking nearby.
4. **Smouldering Corpse**: The charred remains of a fallen adventurer lie half-buried in ash. A collection of d3 Runes can be found, but disturbing the body could set off a trap or alert nearby Flame Spirits.
5. **Volcanic Chamber**: Hidden deep within the mountain, a volcanic chamber awaits. Its walls glow with Runes. Prying them free requires you to Test Might DR12, but failure may cause a violent lava surge.
6. **Petrified Remains**: The stone remains of a creature, burned by volcanic heat, lie frozen in place. They may hold secrets to an ancient civilisation or a cursed item.

## Rumours (d6)

1. "Eldrimnir fortifies the realm." The fire giant Eldrimnir is preparing for war. Building walls of obsidian and forging bridges over the molten rivers, he is reshaping Muspelheim into a fortress of flame. Some whisper he's unlocked the power of fire runes, making his army unstoppable.
2. "Logi seeks a challenge." Logi, the spirit of fire, grows restless in his eternal dominion. He seeks worthy opponents, mortals and gods alike, to test his flames. He's been seen wandering the molten fields, looking for someone brave—or foolish—enough to face him.
3. "The flames hold secrets." It's whispered that the molten rivers of Muspelheim are more than they seem. Some claim the lava holds memories—glimpses of the past or future. But many who've gazed too long have lost their sanity, consumed by the searing visions.
4. "The fire giants are warring." Not all fire giants are united. Rival clans vie for control of the central volcano, and their internal strife has led to whispers of bloodshed. Adventurers may find opportunity in this chaos—alliances, betrayals, or weakness to exploit.
5. "The Ashen Constructs are growing." The Ashen Constructs are multiplying, their presence becoming more widespread. Their growing numbers signal something darker on the horizon.
6. "The volcano is ready to blow." The central volcano of Muspelheim trembles with growing instability. Rumours swirl that a catastrophic eruption is imminent, one that could reshape the realm and send shock waves through the Nine Realms. The ground itself is on the brink of splitting open.

## Encounters (d8)

1. **Flame Spirits**: A swarm of d6 Flame Spirits erupts from the molten rivers, their erratic leaps setting everything they touch ablaze.
2. **Ashen Construct Trap**: The air grows heavy as d4 Ashen Constructs rise from the ashes, reforming into walls and weapons that block the party's path.
3. **Molten Hound Pack**: From a volcanic cave, d6 Molten Hounds burst forth, their claws burning with inner fire. The hounds are relentless, pursuing any who flee.
4. **Inferno Beetle Ambush**: d6 Inferno Beetles explode from the ground, spewing burning acid. Their fire-resistant shells are valuable for crafting heat-resistant gear.
5. **Lava Burst**: The ground trembles as a sudden eruption shoots lava from a fissure, flooding the area with molten rock. The party must Test Swift DR12 to avoid being engulfed. Failure results in d6 fire damage and potential loss of d20x Trinkets to the lava.
6. **Steam Vent Trap**: A hidden steam vent bursts open, releasing a blast of scalding steam. The party must Test Fortitude DR12 to endure the heat, suffering d6 damage on failure. The vent may also reveal a hidden passage beneath the earth, though exploring it risks further eruptions.
7. **Molten River Crossing**: A river of molten lava blocks the way. The party must Test Wits DR12 to find a safe crossing, or take d8 damage from the burning heat.
8. **Flame Tornado**: A flame tornado spirals across the landscape, consuming all in its path. The party must Test Swift DR14 to avoid being caught in the fiery winds, which deal d10 damage and set anything flammable ablaze. The tornado uncovers hidden areas as it tears through the terrain.

## Creatures of Muspelheim

### Flame Spirits
- **HP:** 4
- **Armour:** -
- **Weapon:** Flame touch d4 fire damage
- **Special:** Touch of Flames. On a hit, the target ignites, causing d2 damage per round until extinguished. Explosion. When a Flame Spirit is defeated, it explodes in a burst of fire. All creatures close to it must Test Swift DR12 or take d6 fire damage.

### Ashen Constructs
- **HP:** 7
- **Armour:** Shifting ash - d2
- **Weapon:** Ashen blades d6

### Molten Hounds
- **HP:** 10
- **Armour:** Volcanic hide - d3
- **Weapon:** Molten bite d8
- **Special:** Melt. Fumbles with Molten Bite melt through non-enchanted armour, reducing its effectiveness by an additional 1 tier. Territorial Rage. When defending their territory, Molten Hounds deal +2 damage.

### Inferno Beetles
- **HP:** 6
- **Armour:** Hardened shell - d4
- **Weapon:** Chitinous charge d6 (and knocked prone)
- **Special:** Acid Spray. Once per encounter, the Inferno Beetle can spray burning acid. All targets caught in the spray must Test Swift DR14 or take d6 acid damage and have armour degraded by 1 tier.

### Logi, the Spirit of Fire
- **HP:** 22
- **Armour:** Veil of fire - d2
- **Weapon:** Fire claws d8+3
- **Special:** Unstable Flames. At the start of each round, Logi's chaotic fire spreads to any flammable object within close range, which ignites and burns uncontrollably. Inferno Burst. Once per battle, Logi releases a burst of fire, forcing everyone close to him to Test Swift DR14 or take d8 fire damage.
- **Reward:** The Spirit of the Flame. Activate once to become immune to fire damage for d4 rounds.

### Eldrimnir, Fire Jötunn
- **HP:** 28
- **Armour:** Volcanic rock - d3
- **Weapon:** Molten hammer d10+4
- **Special:** Lava Command. Eldrimnir can summon a river of lava, creating a barrier or line of molten rock. Anyone attempting to cross it takes d10 damage unless they succeed in a Swift Test DR14. Seismic Stomp. Eldrimnir stomps the ground, creating a shock wave. All enemies must Test Swift DR14 or be knocked prone and take d6 damage from the impact.
- **Reward:** Molten Hammer (d10+4 damage).
